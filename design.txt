= Design =
Quest's design

== Controls ==

Strings of numbers as input. Digit = command, chains possible.
Illegal command stops chain.
Return will repeat previous command chain (which is displayed just in case).

== Character ==

Strength -> Damage | Weightlifting
Dexterity -> Aim | Lockpicking
Endurance -> HP | Climbing

=== Items ===

Items enhance by math.floor(factor * player.attribute)
Weapons enhance damage, eyewear (glasses) enhances aim?, armor enhances health?
